Civil Unions

I perform my civic duties to marry all in accordance with my personal values and federal values, whether that be between a man and a woman, or any other gender. I do not discriminate and welcome all to show their dedication and life-long commitment to each other.

Vow Renewals

I love vow renewals and believe they are essential in every marriage. I believe this so much that I will do your vow renewals for free every 5 years after your wedding ceremony.
